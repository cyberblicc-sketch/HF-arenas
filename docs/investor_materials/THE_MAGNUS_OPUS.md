## 4. THE PSYCHOLOGICAL UX & TECH MOAT
Web3 UX is notoriously terrible. We have engineered a trading terminal that feels exactly like Web2 Robinhood, but settles on Web3 infrastructure.

*   **Session Keys (Zero Friction):** Users sign *one* transaction per 24 hours. Our NestJS relayer holds a temporary sub-key, allowing users to rapid-fire click "Buy YES/NO" without a single MetaMask popup interrupting their flow state.
*   **Gasless Execution:** Our backend Gelato relayer subsidizes all gas fees. Users never need MATIC or ETH to trade; they only need USDC.
*   **UMA Optimistic Oracle:** Outcomes are resolved programmatically via Hugging Face APIs, secured by decentralized dispute resolution. No centralized point of failure.
